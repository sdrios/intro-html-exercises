This example is about making lists

Here's what I like about lists:

  • Theyre useful
  • They provide structure
  • They look nice

When you make a list, do it in this order:

  1. Decide if its ordered or not
  2. If it's not ordered, use the ul tag, otherwise use the ol tag
  3. Use li tags nested in the list for your list items
  4. You can nnest lists inside each other!
  5. You got this!
